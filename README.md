# 📊 Zomato Delivery Operations Analysis

## 📌 Project Overview
This project analyzes Zomato delivery operations data to identify key performance metrics, operational inefficiencies, and business insights. The goal is to improve delivery performance, reduce delays, and optimize overall customer experience.

---

## 🛠 Tools & Technologies
- Microsoft Excel (Data Cleaning & Preprocessing)
- SQL (Data Analysis & Querying)
- Power BI (Dashboard & Data Visualization)

---

## 🔄 Project Workflow
1. Cleaned and transformed raw data using Excel (handled missing values, standardized formats)
2. Performed SQL analysis to extract meaningful insights:
   - Order distribution
   - Delivery time analysis
   - Revenue trends
   - Cancellation patterns
3. Built an interactive Power BI dashboard to visualize KPIs and insights

---

## 📊 Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)
- **Total Revenue:** 25M  
- **Total Orders:** 45.58K  
- **Success Rate:** 69.75%  
- **Average Delivery Time:** 26.3 minutes  

---

## 📈 Key Insights
- Metropolitan cities contribute the highest number of orders compared to urban and semi-urban areas  
- Delivery time significantly increases during high traffic conditions (especially in traffic jams)  
- UPI is the most preferred payment method, generating the highest revenue  
- Around 30% of orders are either delayed or cancelled, indicating operational inefficiencies  
- Motorcycles provide faster delivery compared to other vehicle types  

---

## 💡 Business Recommendations
- Optimize delivery partner allocation during peak traffic hours  
- Focus on reducing delays in high-traffic zones  
- Improve operational efficiency to increase success rate beyond 70%  
- Promote digital payments (UPI) for faster transactions  
- Use efficient vehicle allocation strategies for quicker deliveries  

---
